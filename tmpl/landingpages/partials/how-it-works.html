How Knownly Works

1: Link your Dropbox

During signup you will be directed to Dropbox. Log in and give Knownly permissions to create and access a new folder where your website will live. We don't access any other files.

2: Claim your domain

A free Knownly.net sub-domain will be created automatically so you can try things out straight away. You can also claim a domain you already own and after a quick DNS change you're all set.

3: Drop in your files and you're live

Now it's time to design and build your site. Work with one of our templates or create something of your very own.

If you need any assistance along the way we're more than happy to help.

For bigger projects or if you need assistance with frontend design and development, that's an option too.
