About the site

Hi, my name is Dwight and I'm the guy behind Knownly.

I first had the idea to host websites straight from Dropbox back in January 2014. I was reading through the Dropbox API docs for an unrelated project and the light bulb went on...

It would be a great way to quickly put up landing pages for my new ideas. I got excited about doing away with all the Wordpress blogs I'd been sucked in to managing over the years and was sick of maintaining. I also figured it might be useful to other creatives, designers and developers (plus anybody wanting to learn these skills) who don't want to worry about servers and systems admin.

After diving in, the service came together quite quickly. I'd built just enough to scratch my own itch and let in some friends and family to try it out. As happens, other projects came up and I've had Knownly on the shelf until recently.

So it's time to launch properly - to make it known. As I said, I hope it's useful to you and I love to hear feedback :)
